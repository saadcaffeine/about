About

Lab Notebook, Not a LinkedIn.

Equal parts barista, biologist, and systems thinker. I founded SalvageGarden in 2018 as a space where broken things become building blocks — where e-waste becomes educational kit and discarded hardware becomes assistive technology.

My practice lives at the art-science boundary: co-creating tactile games with disability communities, fermenting SCOBY bioplastics, running microbial fuel cell workshops, and building open-source braille printers across Southeast Asia.

I believe open source is infrastructure for democracy. The recipe should always be shared.
